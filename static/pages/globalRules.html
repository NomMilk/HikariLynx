Rules [Last updated: 8/3/2023]

By using this website, you agree that you'll follow the rules of this site. These rules are simple to follow, so if for whatever reason, you violate any of them, your access to the site may be terminated. Also note that moderators have final say and may revoke access or remove content for any reason without notice.

Free speech is not our primary focus. While we're not against it, there are better places for that. Generally, you don't necessarily have to be nice all the time, but don't make things heated.

Moderation

Depending on the content of the post, your post will either stay up with a text that says:

(USER WAS BANNED FOR THIS POST)

or the entire post may be deleted.

Once you are banned, you will still be able to access the website, but unable to post.

Global Rules

1. You must be 18 years of age or older to browse this website.

2. You will not upload, post, receive, or transmit content that violates local or United States law. This site does not provide a platform for illegal activity of any kind.

3. Any media that depicts the sexual abuse of children is explicitly forbidden.

  • This includes any kind of jailbait/borderline content
  • Even if fictional, realistic and/or 3DCG depictions are also forbidden

4. Respect the rules of the board owners

5. Do not post any NSFW content on SFW boards (Includes anything sexual/grotesque)

  • Light ecchi is allowed to be unspoilered if it does not include le penis and le vagen, or bewbs
  • If it only shows any private body part, it should be spoiled
  • Any media depicting blatantly sexual acts will be deleted

6. Spam or advertisements to unrelated services/sites will be deleted

  • If you host a board, and would like to hotlink (Added to our friends page): Post on >>>/h3/ about your site.

7. Do not evade bans or abuse the report system

8. You will not post the following:

  • Political posts made to arouse a debate
  • Irrelevant posts made to derail a board/thread, or any made to simply be annoy other users
  • Anything that is excessively distasteful/deragatory (Including irrevelant, violent, unnecessary and troll posts)

  • Pepe or Wojacks of any variation
  • Buzzwords and most Nu-internet slang (Not outright banned, but should be kept minimum)

Board Creation/Managing Rules

1. Do not make an excessive amount of boards. This includes duplicates, similar, etc

2. A board may be deleted if no new posts are made for 4 weeks

3. A board may also be deleted if it violates any of our global rules

4. Your board may be transferred to a new owner if you do not log on after 2 weeks

5. The board name, description, and tags must be relevant to the discussion

6. If your board is on an NSFW topic, you cannot mark it as SFW

7. You cannot create a board for the following topics:

  • Specific fetishes (e.g. feet, tickling, etc) - Exceptions can be made if you ask in /h3/
  • Politics/Politically Incorrect (don't make anything that resembles /pol/ here)
  • Another "random" (Just use /b/)
  • A board made with the purpose of leading users to another website
  • Harassment campaigns
  • Boards for different regions/languages - We will lift this rule eventually

8. If majority of users do not like your board, we will likely delete it

9. Do not be hostile towards other boards.
