General
  • What is hikari3.ch?

Hikari3.ch is an image-based forum (imageboard), where users can have discussions on a variety of topics, with more emphasis on images. Boards are user created and run, so topics can range from anime, video games, to literature and technology. Account registration is not needed for simply discussing, but you will need one if youre managing a board. You can choose to either post anonymously, or use a tripcode as a form of identification.


Our goal is to have a space for anyone to discuss their interests, whether its niche or mainstream, while also keeping it comfy.

  • Who is that blue woman?

It is our mascot named "Hikari". A holographic girl from the future.

Bypassing
  • What is a bypass?

A Bypass is a temporary identifier that gives you access to certain site functions. Most importantly, you need a bypass to post.

A bypass will expire after a set number of hours or after making so many posts. The exact values may vary.

  • What is captcha?

This is a system that requires users to generate a cryptographic hash to receive a bypass for captchas on certain boards. Users may need to generate two captchas if a board requires it. The time to generate a bypass is determined by the user's computer specs and requires the user to keep the tab open until the process is complete. The system is used to prevent spam and may be activated during attacks on the site. Users will be compensated with a longer-lasting bypass and larger post allotment.

Names
  • What is a tripcode?

A tripcode allows users to authenticate their identity without revealing personal information. It works by converting a password into a code, which is then displayed next to the user's name or post. This can be used to verify the identity of the user across different threads or boards. In some cases, a secure tripcode may be used to prevent others from generating the same code and impersonating the user.

  • How do I use a tripcode?

To use a tripcode, add a # in your name. After the #, add the password for the tripcode. The server will apply a hash to the password and display the hashed password. To use a secure tripcode, add ## in your name and a password. A secure tripcode will generate a random text to be displayed that will only be used if your password matches. You can also enable an always use role signature rule in your account settings.

  • What is a role signature?

If you wish to display your global or board role when posting, you can put #rs in your name, like a tripcode, case insensitive. This will prevent you from using a tripcode.
