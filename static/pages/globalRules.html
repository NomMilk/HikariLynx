Global Rules

1. You must be 18 years of age or older to browse this website.

To access or browse this website, users must be at least 18 years of age or older. This age restriction is in place to ensure that the website's content is appropriate for adult audiences.

2. You will not upload, post, receive, or transmit content that violates local or United States law. This site does not provide a platform for illegal activity of any kind.

This includes but is not limited to content that is defamatory, abusive, harassing, threatening, or infringes on the intellectual property rights of others.

3. All “SFW” boards are to be considered "work safe". Do not post any NSFW content on these boards.

“SFW” stands for Safe For Work. This means that all content posted on this website should be appropriate for a professional setting and not contain any explicit or inappropriate material.

4. Do not request/post personal information (“dox”)

Users are not allowed to request or post personal information about others, also known as “doxing”. This includes but is not limited to names, addresses, phone numbers, and social security numbers.

5. Do not spam and/or advertise.

Users are not allowed to spam or advertise on this website. This includes but is not limited to posting unsolicited advertisements, links to other websites, or promoting products or services without permission.

If you would like to advertise officially, email info@hikari3.ch

6. Do not abuse report system.

Users are not allowed to abuse the report system by filing false reports or repeatedly filing reports against other users without justification.

7. Do not impersonate administration.

Users are not allowed to impersonate administrators or moderators of the website. This includes using their names to deceive others into thinking that they are official representatives of the website.

8. You will not post the following outside of /b/:

  • Troll posts
  • Excessively derogatory posts
  • Furry porn
  • Guro images
  • Loli/shota porn
  • Politics
